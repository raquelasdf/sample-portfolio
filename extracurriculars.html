For the show A Very Potter Musical, I textured and painted the 12 X 20 foot backdrop as well as assisted in the construction of the arch. To texture the foam backdrop I experimented with covering portions with tape and melting the foam with a heat gun. I then mixed the paint and spent many hours painting the entire backdrop.

RENT Set Spiral Staircase

March 16-18, 2017 Extracurriculars

For the production of RENT, my technical director delegated me the task of building a safe and structural spiral staircase. We completed many computations to calculate the width and number of steps.

I then 3D modeled the shape of the step and manufactured them using a CNC router. With assistance of the set construction crew, we assembled the staircase and tested it for stability.

RENT Marketing Campaign

March 16-18, 2017 Extracurricular

For my school's production of RENT I coordinated the marketing team to lead a sucessful campaign to sell out tickets for almost every night. I ordered and distributed gutair picks to local music schools and guitar stores. This let us target an audience that would be more interested in the type of music in the show RENT.

I coordinated a school wide program to inform the student body about some of the more sensitive topics featured in RENT to prepare them for the show. This included an interactive program where students would take on the roles of different members of the community and react to scenarios. This exercise opened students' minds to different perspectives to help them become more kind and empathetic.

Aida Marketing Campaign

March 10-12 2016 Extracurricular

To market my school’s production of Aida, we hid 17 custom 3-D printed Aida pyramids throughout the school. If a pyramid was returned to the director’s room, the student would receive a coupon for a free snack and drink during intermission. Students loved the challenge and it definitely got people talking about the show.
