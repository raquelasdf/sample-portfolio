Under Bed Closet

September 2017 Projects

When faced with the problem of not enough drawer space in my dorm, I decided to engineer a better solution. I designed and fabricated two aluminum hooks to hold a PVC pipe and serve as a closet underneath my excessively tall bed. The hooks wrap around a bar on the small bookshelf under my bed and the weight of my clothes bends the hooks in a way that prevents them from sliding off.

To bend the metal to make the hooks, first I created 2 test pieces to become comfortable with using the metal finger brake. (Yes, that is the real name of the machine.)

I then tested these pieces and used a thicker piece of aluminum to make the final hooks. This quick invention has greatly improved my organization as my shirts have a home and are less wrinkly than being shoved in a drawer.
